Tiramisu Recipe

Description

THE Italian dessert made of custard, whipped cream, coffee and overall delicious goodness

Ingredients

  • Egg Yolks
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Vanilla
  • Coffee
  • Ladyfingers
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Make the filling

    2. Cook the egg yolk, sugar and milk until slightly thickened. Then let it chill in the fridge for at least one hour.

  2. Make the whipped cream

    3. Beat heavy cream with vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

  3. Soak the ladyfingers

    4. Combine the coffee and rum into a small bowl. Then pour the mixture over ladyfingers.

  4. Assemble the Tiramisu

    5. Line the bottom of a baking dish with soaked lady fingers. Sperad the creamy mixture over the ladyfingers and then whipped cream over that. Repeat in that order.Finall top it off with a generous amount of cocoa powder.
